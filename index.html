Craig Federighi

SVP of Software Engineering at Apple Inc.

Career Timeline

  • NeXT

    Year Unknown

    Craig Federighi worked under Steve Jobs in his time at NeXT, he led the development of the Enterprise Objects Framework.

  • Apple

    1996

    He joined Apple when it axquired NeXT in 1996.

  • Ariba

    1999

    He joined Ariba, the enterprise IT company, where he was Chief Technology Officer.

  • Apple

    2009

    He returned to Apple, hired to lead macOS engineering. In March 2011, he succeeded Bertrand Serlet as vice president of Mac Software Engineering at Apple, and in August 2012 he was promoted to senior vice president, reporting to CEO Tim Cook. Upon Scott Forstall's departure from Apple, his role was expanded to encompass iOS in addition to macOS.
